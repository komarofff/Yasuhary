Description

Featuring a 4 bedroom, 3 bathroom home in a quiet neighborhood of Porter Ranch. This 2 story home sits on a massive lot of over 11,700 sqft, has a gated front yard and an attached 3 car garage. The living room has carpet flooring, recessed lighting throughout, and large windows letting in ample amount of natural light. To the right of entry, find the formal dining room and kitchen right adjacent to it with the family room. The kitchen has concrete countertops, stainless steel appliances, including a Viking range, and lots of cabinet space for storage. The family room has laminate flooring and a fireplace, along with sliding glass doors leading out to the back yard. Through the hall find a wet bar, a bathroom with a shower stall with an exit to the back yard, and the laundry area with storage. All the bedrooms are up the stairs and all have carpet flooring and ceiling fans to keep cool. The primary bedroom has a walk in closet and the bathroom has dual sinks with a shower stall. The other bedrooms share a bathroom in the hall that has double sinks and a shower over tub. The entertainers backyard has a large pool with a diving board to keep cool in the summer and lots of space to host guests!
